Festival Recipes

Ugadi Pachadi

Of this pachadi (or Ugadi pacchadi) is most notable, and consists of a chutney-like dish which includes ingredients to give all flavors: sweet, sour, tangy and bitter.

Poornalu

Poornalu is the traditional Indian sweet in the Telugu festivals. It is made of rice flour stuffed with jaggery mixed dal paste, dry fruits. It is often served hot with ghee.

Sweet Pongal

Chakkara pongal (literally sweet pongal) is generally prepared in temples as a prasadam. This type of pongal is also made during Pongal / Sankranti in Andhra Pradesh

Kajjikayalu

Karjikaya is an authentic Sweet deeply Fried Andhra Empanada, filled with sugar, fried gram, cashews,almonds and pumpkin seeds.Karjikaya is made during special occasions like Vinayaka chaturthi

Minapa Garelu

Poornalu is the traditional Indian sweet in the Telugu festivals. It is made of rice flour stuffed with jaggery mixed dal paste, dry fruits. It is often served hot with ghee.festivals ends without this Minupa garelu.

Chekkalu

Wheat Chekkalu is made with wheat flour and some nuts. This can be made during krishna jayanthi and deepavali. Making chekkalu with wheat flour is an authentic vysyas Recipe.
